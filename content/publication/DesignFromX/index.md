abstract: "This paper presents a consumer-oriented approach to design space exploration by structuring the feature composition of existing products. Instead of generating designs from scratch, the system extracts and organizes reusable product features, allowing users to explore design alternatives through feature recombination and modification. The work emphasizes the value of structured references and compositional exploration for non-expert design participation."
